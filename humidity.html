Latitude vs. Humidity

SUMMARY: When examining Latitude vs. Humidity, the realtionship (as observed) was weak. There appears to be no visible relationship as one travels away from the equator between latitude and humidity. Humidity is known to be related to other factors such as altitude and temperature. It is known that relative humidity changes when temperatures change. Air temperature changes affect how much water vapor the air can hold and values such as relative humidity and dew point help describe these effects on weather. While locations with the highest humidity are generally located closer to the equator, near coastal regions it likley due to the interaction of mulitple factors and not just the single factor of latitude.
